# **App Name**: Cartas para Helena

## Core Features:

- Book Information Display: Present the book cover, title, author, comprehensive description, key quotes, and product benefits clearly on a dedicated landing page.
- Edition & Price Selection: Allow users to choose between available book editions (e.g., 'eBook', 'eBook + Cartas Inéditas') and display their respective prices.
- Quantity Adjustment: Enable users to easily increase or decrease the desired quantity for their selected book edition.
- Add-on Product Selection: Offer additional related books by the same author as optional purchases, with dynamic updating of the order total when selected.
- Dynamic Order Summary: Display a real-time, detailed summary of all chosen products, their quantities, individual prices, subtotals, add-on costs, and the grand total.
- Customer Email Input: Provide a form for securely collecting the customer's email address, which will be used for digital product delivery and order confirmation.
- AI-Powered Letter Snippet Generator: Provide a tool where users can generate short, personalized letter snippets inspired by the romantic and reflective tone of 'Cartas para Helena,' based on their input themes or keywords, to encourage engagement and illustrate the book's style.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, muted plum (#51405A), conveying depth, introspection, and warmth fitting for a memorial romance.
- Background color: A very light, subtly purple-gray (#F7F5F7), providing a soft, elegant, and harmonious base for the overall design, visually connected to the primary hue.
- Accent color: A soft rose-pink (#CC7AA8), analogous to the primary hue, offering a gentle yet distinct contrast for highlights, calls to action, and emphasizing romantic elements.
- Headline font: 'Literata' (serif), selected for its elegant, literary, and vintage aesthetic, ideal for titles, quotes, and primary textual emphasis.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities, suitable for all descriptive text, UI elements, and general content.
- Utilize clean, outline-style icons from the Lucide library to maintain a sophisticated, minimal, and consistent visual language throughout the interface.
- A responsive two-column layout on larger screens, dedicating ample space to book details on one side and the ordering process on the other. This transitions to a clean, readable single-column flow on mobile devices, emphasizing clarity and user focus.
- Implement subtle hover effects on interactive elements such as buttons and edition selections, along with smooth transitions for quantity adjustments and dynamic content updates, to provide gentle user feedback.
